Polytechnic State University of Bicol

Read Edit Customize History Related Pages

The Polytechnic State University of Bicol (PSUB) is a state university in Nabua, Camarines Sur, Philippines. It is mandated primarily to provide higher technological, professional, and vocational instruction and training in fisheries, trade, as well as short-term technical and vocational courses, as the Board of Trustees may deem necessary, and shall promote research in the exploration and conservation of natural resources in the province. [2] Its main campus is located in Nabua, Camarines Sur. [3]

Conception

Polytechnic State University of Bicol was born out of the Camarines Sur Polytechnic Colleges, popularly known as CSPC. CSPC was the brainchild of some prominent Nabueños who sought to establish a higher education institution in the region.
